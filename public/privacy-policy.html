Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 March 2026

Welcome to Fala Brazil! ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our language learning app at fala-brazil-m5z1.vercel.app.

By using Fala Brazil!, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our app:

  • Account information: If you create an account, we collect your name and email address.
  • Learning data: Your selected Portuguese level, progress through lessons, and quiz results.
  • Usage data: Pages visited and features used within the app, to help us improve the experience.
  • Device information: Browser type and device type, collected automatically.

If you use Google Sign-In, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google, in accordance with Google's own privacy policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Personalise your learning experience based on your level
  • Save your progress across devices
  • Improve the app's features and content
  • Respond to support requests

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your account data is stored securely using Google Firebase, a cloud platform provided by Google LLC. Firebase stores data on servers located in the European Union and/or the United States. Firebase complies with GDPR and other applicable data protection regulations.

If you choose to use the app without creating an account, your progress is stored locally on your device only and is not sent to our servers.

4. AI Features

Fala Brazil! uses Anthropic's Claude AI to power certain features, including the video Q&A assistant and AI Tutor. When you use these features, your question and relevant context (such as the video title and your level) are sent to Anthropic's API to generate a response. We do not store these conversations on our servers. Please refer to Anthropic's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle data.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage to remember your level selection and preferences between sessions. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are based in the European Union or United Kingdom, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access: You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You can ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure: You can ask us to delete your account and all associated data.
  • Right to portability: You can request your data in a portable format.
  • Right to object: You can object to certain uses of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

8. Children's Privacy

Fala Brazil! is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
